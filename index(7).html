EDITING CROP AREA
Drag the clothing photo to position it. Scroll or pinch to zoom. Drag a round corner handle to resize the crop area. The dashed frame is never saved.

On iPhone or iPad, Choose Photo should open the image picker offered by Safari. On a Mac, the browser still controls the Apple file window. The Save button will use the Share Sheet where supported and download the PNG otherwise.
